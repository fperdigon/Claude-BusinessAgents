Usage Rules

Usage Rules

Clear Space

Always leave clear space around the monogram equal to the height of the "F" initial letter. Around the wordmark, leave half the height of the lowercase x-height on all sides. Never crowd the mark with other text or UI elements.

Headshot Crops

Use square (1:1) for primary identity placements — LinkedIn profile, conference badges, About pages. Use circle for icon-scale uses (32–96 px) — apply the circular crop with a 4 px ring in #22d3ee (dark mode) or #0891b2 (light mode).

Photo Grading

Engineer-precise palette pairs with neutral or cool grading — high-key portrait, sharp detail, minimal color cast. Avoid warm-toned filters; they conflict with the cyan accent. On conference slides or banners, use a slight cool desaturation to harmonize with the slate background.

Don't

  • Stretch or distort the monogram or wordmark at any scale.
  • Recolor the monogram outside the palette — only cyan, slate, or white on dark; only cyan-600 or slate-900 on light.
  • Place the wordmark on a busy photo without a translucent slate scrim (minimum 60% opacity).
  • Use the accent cyan as a background color for large areas — it is a highlight color, not a surface color.
  • Mix dark-mode and light-mode assets in the same document — pick one and stay consistent.
